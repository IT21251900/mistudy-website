Research Domain

Understanding the landscape of our research regarding ASD.

Our survey highlights: Traditional LMS limitations: rigidity, lack of personalization, static content. The emergence of AI in education: adaptive learning, intelligent tutoring systems, stealth assessments. Cognitive strategies like the Pomodoro technique, mind mapping, and cognitive load theory. Gaps in integrating real-time feedback, dynamic quizzes, and intelligent session tracking.
